About Us

Our Inspiration and Foundation

Neocycle is an upcycling store focused on delivering sustainable products to build a cleaner world. Our journey began with a clear vision - to make a positive impact on the world while offering quality products. The idea stemmed from a deep concern for the environment and a burning desire to make a difference. Our founder, Pedro Daniel, had a moment of epiphany witnessing the damage caused by plastic waste in our community. This experience inspired the creation of Neocycle, where we transform recyclable materials into functional and aesthetically pleasing products.

At Neocycle, our mission is clear: to reduce waste and promote a more sustainable lifestyle.
